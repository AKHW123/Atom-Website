4. Drew Doughty - 91 Overall

Coming from a Kings fan, there is no way Drew Doughty should be tied for the best defenseman in the league based on overall rating. His best days are behind him, and even though you could say that he just plays for a bad team, he is still way overrated by EA. Despite playing on the top defensive pair all season long, he finished at a +/- of -16 and served 36 penalty minutes, which was the fourth highest on the whole team. However, maybe a deserves a 99 Overall for his chirp game.

2. Braden Holtby - 88 Overall

This one is just a no-brainer. Holtby had a terrible season that ended with him getting traded, and he has no reason to be in the top 10 goalies this year. He finished the season with a save percentage of less than .900, and he gave up an average of 3.11 goals per game. 3.11. That puts him in the bottom half of all goalies this year. And he played for a good team that just won the cup.

1. Cale Makar - 85 Overall

Despite winning the Calder Trophy, he isn't even the best rated rookie this year, which makes absolutely no sense. He finished the regular season with 12 goals and 50 points and a +/- of +12, despite only playing 57 games AS A ROOKIE DEFENSEMAN. On top of that, he only had 12 penalty minutes all season, and he put up an average of one point per game during the playoffs. AS A ROOKIE DEFENSEMAN. This one is completely ridiculous, and it has earned the #1 spot.
